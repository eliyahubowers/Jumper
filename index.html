Jumper

by: Elijah Bowers

This game was made for my level 2 project at The League of Amazing Programmers.
Click here to download and play!

1. Why I Made This Game

I made this game because it seemed simple but that there were many interesting ways for me to change it.

2. What I Learned

My teaches and classmates helped me with the instructions for the game and the length of the timer.

3. Architecture of My Game

In my game you are trying to get to the other side of the river like frogger. Except when you land in the river you don't die but instead get pushed to one of the sides of the screen where you die. Also the logs all move in the opposite direction and if you go off the screen that way you also die.

4. Technical Implementation

One thing that I really like about the game is that the logs move you slower to the left than the river brings you to the right. This makes people get really impatient and rush themselves so even though the game is easy people make it hard for themselves.

5. Most Challenging Part

One of the things that was hard to create in the game was the visual affect that you are looking at it from an angle. I think that this makes the game look much nicer that if it looked just flat.

6. Future Fixes and Updates

In the future I would want to make the game get progressively harder by making everything move faster.
